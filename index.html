FAQ

What should I wear?

Anything between smart casual and semi-formal, as long as it is church appropriate. The middle ground should be business casual.

Can I bring a +1?

Please check your invite for a +1! Unfortunately, our restaurant is space limited, if you would like to bring your +1, please reach out to us and we will do our best to accommodate! Thank you so much for understanding!

Do I need to be Catholic to attend the Solemnisation?

No! We would love for everyone to witness our Solemnisation.

What kind of meal is provided?

There will be light refreshments provided after the church ceremony. Lunch will be a 4-course Italian meal. Meal selection will be sent at a later date, special dietary restrictions can be accommodated for.

How do I get to the church/restaurant?

Please see directions here. A bus will be chartered between the two locations, please indicate if you need the transport in your RSVP.

Will there be a livestream if I cannot make it for the event?

Unfortunately no ):

Can I bring my cat?

No. Both the church and restaurant are not pet-friendly establishments ):
